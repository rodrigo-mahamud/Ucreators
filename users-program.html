All La Latina’s teachers are native speakers from Spain and Hispanic America.

All our teachers have university degrees in Education and training in specific fields related to the teaching of Spanish as a foreign language. Our team also comprises of teachers with education in specific fields like Law and Economics, being specialists in teaching Spanish for Business. They all share the same passion and enthusiasm for teaching Spanish.

You can purchase a pack of lessons on our online shop or you can also send as an email (onlinelalatina@gmail.com) with your questions and we will then respond to them as soon as possible.

Once we receive the tuition payment for your desired lessons voucher we will make an appointment for your first lesson. Lessons can take place on weekdays from 7am to 9pm GTM+1.

The lessons will take place on Skype, although other platforms like Zoom can be used on demand.

Lessons are 60, 90 or 120 minutes long.
We offer 4 lesson packages: 7, 14, 24, 32 hours
For instance, with a 24 x 60 minutes lessons package you can get:
24 lessons 60 minutes each
16 lessons 90 minutes each
12 lessons 120 minutes each
The number of classes per week a student can take is unlimited.

If you are a complete beginner a level test is not necessary. If, on the contrary, you do not know your level, you will take a level test in advance online

The language used in our classes is Spanish. Even for the very beginners, our teachers are well qualified to do so. A high level of English of the student is not required.

Our classes follow our own Bullidor teaching method. Each student can have classes with different teachers throughout the duration of the course. Our method consists of following a course syllabus, i.e. materials, topics, exercises, etc.. Every class is very dynamic and fun as well. Every student will not only learn how to speak Spanish, but enjoy the whole process of learning a new language.

Throughout the course we will be using exercises corresponding to each level. All the materials are included in the price of the course. The student will receive them during the course.

Once a level is completed an end of course test is done. If the student passes the test they will receive a certificate.

Each course is fully customised according to the needs of each student. Lessons can be focused on one only skill or combine grammar, conversation, Spanish for business, etc.
Beginner A1
Elementary A2
Lower Intermediate B1
Intermediate B2
Upper Intermediate C1
Advanced C2
This is the most demanded option by our students, once the level test has been done and the student’s level has been determined they can start moving forward through the above mentioned level scale.

The course can be for 2 people too. The previous conditions are applicable in this case, although taking into account too that it is mandatory for both students to have the same level and availability.

Once the course is over you can purchase another pack of lessons at our online shop We will contact you by email once we receive the payment..
